# Sitemap Strategy

## 1. Home (`/`)
- **Goal:** Remove friction to attendance.
- **Variants:**
  - **A (Logistics):** Focus on "Next Session" time and clear schedule.
  - **B (Emotion):** Focus on "Fun, Friendly" vibe and removing fear for beginners.

## 2. Timetable (`/timetable`)
- Dedicated page for SEO clarity.
- Mon/Wed/Sat schedule.

## 3. Competitions (`/competitions`)
- Data-driven table fetching from Google Sheets CSV.
- Shows Date, Event, Division, Status.

## 4. Location & Contact (`/location`)
- Combined page for "Find Us".
- Map placeholder, parking info, entry instructions.
- Simple contact form (Formspree).

## 5. What to Expect (`/expect`)
- FAQ style "Fear Reduction".
- Pricing info.
- Linked from Home (Variant B).
