Our Story

The Newcastle PCYC Table Tennis Club has been a fixture of the local sporting community since 1996. For nearly 30 years, we've been bringing people together through the sport we love.

Based inside PCYC Newcastle in Broadmeadow, we offer a welcoming environment for players of all ages and skill levels ‚Äî from complete beginners picking up a bat for the first time, to experienced players looking for competitive matches.

We're proudly affiliated with Table Tennis NSW and registered with Ratings Central, connecting our members to the broader table tennis community across New South Wales and beyond.
